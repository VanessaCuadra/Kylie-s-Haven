Tempura

Ingredients

Dough:

  • jumbo or medium shrimp
  • all-purpose flour
  • cornstarch
  • egg
  • water (icy-cold)

Steps

  1. Boil the mafaldine pasta and salt water according to packaging instruction.
  2. Season your chicken breasts with spices.
  3. Pan fry each side for a few minutes.
  4. In that same pan, add minced garlic, butter, and flour to make a roux.
  5. After achieving a good color on the roux, add heavy cream, bouillon, parmesan cheese and mozzarella cheese.
  6. When the sauce thickens up, add the mafaldine pasta in the sauce and give it a nice toss around.
  7. Serve.
